Results day is here…

You’ve spent weeks working hard on your last assignment and now it’s time to learn whether all your hard work has paid off…

But there’s a problem!

Professor Al Beback, on hearing of a rare Amazonian plant that could aid her research into humans developing the ability to see in the dark, has taken off (University permission still pending)!

All your grades are locked away somewhere on her desk and you need to find them. She’s known to be a little eccentric so it may not be as easy as it sounds.

She has, however, left one clue:

“the road to success starts by turning on the light.”

Odd for someone who wants to see in the dark!

Your Mission

  • Reveal a number string from each puzzle.
  • Enter the code into her lockbox to retrieve your grades!

Work together, explore every clue, and when you're ready – begin your investigation.
